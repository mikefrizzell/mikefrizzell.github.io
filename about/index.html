About

I was born and grew up in suburban Detroit in the 1980s. When I was 7, my parents packed up our family and moved to Queretaro, Mexico. We spent a year there learning Spanish and then moved to Tarija, Bolivia, a city in the heart of South America. My parents, Baptist missionaries, still live there. From there, I moved to rural Michigan, finished high school, got married, and moved to Springfield, Missouri, the Queen City of the Ozarks. For the past 20 years I've called Missouri my home, raising 4 children and 2 dogs here. I am currently a health information management professional for a large health system.
